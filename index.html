MOBA x DUNGEON CRAWLING

- THE GAME -

It's been a thousand years since the Fortress vanished from the world. The portals, once used to join nations, remained unpowered until their powers were no more than legends.


But one day, those tales of legendary Weapons, god-like Crystals and Lost Civilizations suddenly became truth, when the Portals regained their power and led everyone back to a Dungeon frozen in time.
​
​​
In this 3 vs 3 multiplayer game, two teams of explorers shall fight within an ancient fortress in search for the Dungeon Core, a lost source of infinite energy. In their way they shall not only fight each other, but also dangerous monsters that roam the room-based map. To achieve their goal they must master their abilities, level up their Stats and get Roguelike-style powerups!

This original MOBA is reinventing the formula, inspired by our favorite Dungeon-Crawling and Roguelike games. Explore a MOBA-style dungeon with our 6 playable characters, each with unique archetypes and abilities
